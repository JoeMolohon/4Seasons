"Where good food is always in season!"

At Four Seasons Restaurant, we know that good food never gets old. Originally opened in 1940, and reopened in 2019, our recipes have stood the test of time. With that traditional, diner feel, a trip to Four Seasons is bound to be enjoyable for people of all ages. Family-friendly, and with healthy portions of home cookin', Four Seasons hopes to be part of Mahtomedi for years to come.

Our restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We're a great place for a family brunch, or even if you're just looking to stop by for a bite! We have sandwiches, burgers, turkey dinners, pie, and more. Check out our menu for a full list of options. Be sure to try our famous root beer too!

Four Seasons will host special events from time to time. Check out our Facebook page for more information, or reach out to us with a quick message.
